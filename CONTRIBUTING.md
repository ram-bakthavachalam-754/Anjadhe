# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in Anjadhe.

**This is a source-available project, not an open-contribution one.** The code is
published so you can read, study, and audit it — see the [LICENSE](LICENSE)
(PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0) for what use is permitted.

## We are not accepting external contributions

- **Pull requests will not be merged** and may be closed without review.
- **Issues and Discussions are disabled.** The public repository is a one-way
  mirror of releases from a private development repository; changes flow out, not
  in.

## Security

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please **do not open a
public issue.** Email the maintainer at **ram.bakthavachalam@gmail.com** with
details and we will follow up. Responsible disclosure is appreciated.

## Why source-available?

Anjadhe is a privacy-first personal assistant that runs open-weight models
locally. Publishing the source lets anyone verify those privacy claims —
what the app does with your data, what leaves your machine — without opening the
project to redistribution or commercial reuse.
